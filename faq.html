General Questions

What is DevTemple?

DevTemple is a marketplace for web templates, code snippets, and development services. We connect developers who create digital products with buyers who need them for their projects.

How do I get started with DevTemple?

Getting started is simple:

  • Create an account with your name, email, etc.
  • Browse our marketplace for templates and code snippets
  • Purchase items using our secure payment system
  • If you're a creator, you can also upload your own products to sell
What are the Community Guidelines?

Our Community Guidelines ensure a safe and respectful environment:

  • Treat others with respect - no harassment or hate speech
  • Upload only original or properly licensed content
  • Provide accurate product descriptions and previews
  • No fake reviews or rating manipulation
  • Report suspicious activity or security issues
  • No promotion of violence, illegal activities, or IP theft
  • Maintain one account per person
  • Get approval before posting advertisements

Accounts & Profiles

How do I create an account?

To create an account, you'll need to provide:

  • Your full name
  • A valid email address
  • Your bio, photo and skill
  • A secure password

Passwords are encrypted and never stored in plaintext for security.

What are my account responsibilities?

As a DevTemple user, you're responsible for:

  • Keeping your login credentials secure
  • Reporting any unauthorized access immediately
  • Ensuring your account information is accurate and up-to-date
  • Complying with our Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy
  • Following all applicable laws (NDPR, GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
Can I have multiple accounts?

No, we enforce a "One Account, One You" policy. Creating multiple accounts is against our Community Guidelines and may result in account suspension.

How do I update my profile information?

You can update your profile information through your account settings. It's important to keep your details current, especially your email address, to receive important notifications about your purchases and account.

For Buyers

How do I purchase items on DevTemple?

To purchase items:

  • Browse our marketplace and find products you like
  • Review product descriptions, images, and previews carefully
  • Add items to your cart
  • Proceed to checkout and complete payment via Paystack
  • Do not close the checkout window until payment is fully processed
  • After successful payment, you'll be redirected back to DevTemple
What should I check before making a purchase?

Before purchasing any item, make sure to:

  • Read the product description thoroughly
  • Examine the cover image and any preview materials
  • Check the license terms for usage rights
  • Verify compatibility with your projects
  • Review seller ratings and feedback
What if I encounter issues with a purchased item?

If you experience problems with a purchased item:

  • First, contact the seller directly through our messaging system
  • If the issue isn't resolved, report harmful or malicious content using the report button on the product page
  • For technical issues preventing access, contact our support team
  • Refunds may be considered for inaccessible content due to technical issues

For Sellers

How do I become a seller on DevTemple?

To become a seller:

  • Create a DevTemple account if you haven't already
  • Navigate to the seller dashboard
  • Complete the seller verification process
  • Start uploading your original or properly licensed content
What is the 30% service charge for sellers?

DevTemple applies a 30% service charge on each sale. This commission covers:

  • Platform maintenance and development
  • Payment processing fees
  • Customer support services
  • Marketing and marketplace promotion
  • Security and data protection measures

For example, if you sell a product for $10, you'll receive $7 after our commission.

How and when do I get paid?

Payout details for sellers:

  • Payouts are processed after you submit a withdrawal request.
  • Payments follow our regular payout schedule (usually once a month).
  • Earnings are calculated after deducting a 30% service fee.
  • Payouts may be delayed or withheld if an account or sale is under review for policy violations.

If you don’t receive your payout or notice any issues, please contact our support team within 30 days.

What are the content requirements for sellers?

As a seller, you must:

  • Upload only original or properly licensed content
  • Provide accurate product descriptions
  • Include a clear cover image representing the product
  • Use screenshots or previews that show the actual product output
  • Avoid misleading visuals or unrelated stock images
  • Price products fairly according to their value
  • Follow our Community Guidelines and Terms of Service
What should I do to get verified?

After signing up, go to your dashboard and complete your personal information, then click “Save Changes.” Once we review and confirm your details, your account will be verified.

You can still sell without verification, but getting verified helps you stand out — a verified badge will appear on your posts, showing buyers that you’re a trusted seller. Verified sellers often gain more visibility and buyer confidence.

Why should I get verified?

Getting verified helps you build trust and credibility on the platform. A verified badge shows buyers that your account details have been reviewed and confirmed, making you look more professional and reliable.

Verified sellers often get more visibility, attract more buyers, and enjoy faster sales because customers feel more confident dealing with them.

What is verification?

Verification is the process of confirming your identity and account details to ensure authenticity and safety for all users. It helps maintain a secure and trustworthy marketplace by verifying that sellers are real and their information is accurate.

Once verified, a badge appears on your profile and posts to show buyers you’re an approved and trusted seller.

Privacy & Security

What information does DevTemple collect?

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and country
  • Payment Information: Payment history via Paystack to track transactions
  • Usage Data: Cookies, session data, and analytics to improve user experience

Passwords are never stored in plaintext - only encrypted versions are maintained for security.

How is my data protected?

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of sensitive information (passwords, payment details)
  • Secure handling of payment data by Paystack
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited access to personal data for authorized personnel only

However, no system is completely secure, so users should also protect their account credentials.

What are my privacy rights?

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Receive your data in a portable format (GDPR)
  • Opt out of data sharing (we don't sell your data)
  • Restrict processing in certain circumstances
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

How are cookies used on DevTemple?

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Managing user sessions and core functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Analyzing platform performance and usage
  • Functional Cookies: Providing personalized content and recommendations

Under GDPR, we require explicit consent for non-essential cookies. You can manage preferences via the cookie consent popup or browser settings.

Payments & Refunds

What payment methods are accepted?

DevTemple uses Paystack for payment processing, which supports various payment methods including:

  • Credit and debit cards
  • Bank transfers
  • Mobile money (in supported regions)
  • Other local payment options depending on your country
What is your refund policy?

Our refund policy:

  • Payments are generally non-refundable
  • Refunds may be considered if technical issues prevent access to purchased content
  • If Paystack experiences issues (failed debit, double charge), they handle refunds according to their policies
  • Refunds may be approved if content is proven fraudulent, harmful, or misrepresented
  • Disputes must be reported within 30 days of purchase

We encourage buyers to review products carefully before purchasing.

What happens if I close the payment window too early?

If you close the checkout window before payment is fully processed:

  • Your purchase may not be recorded
  • It can be difficult to trace or refund incomplete transactions
  • You might be charged without receiving the product

Always wait for the payment confirmation and redirection back to DevTemple before closing any windows.

Who should I contact for payment issues?

For payment-related issues:

  • First, check your transaction history in your account
  • If the issue is with Paystack (failed payments, double charges), contact Paystack support
  • For other payment issues or if you didn't receive purchased content, contact DevTemple support
  • Include transaction details and screenshots when reporting issues
