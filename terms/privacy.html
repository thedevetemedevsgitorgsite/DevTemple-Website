DevTemple — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18th of September, 2025

At DevTemple, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our website, marketplace, and services (“Platform”). By using the Platform, you consent to the practices described in this policy, subject to your rights under applicable laws such as GDPR and CCPA.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use the Platform:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and country information.
  • Payment Information: Payment history, including purchases made via paystack, to track transactions and manage licenses.
  • Usage Data: Cookies, session data, usage analytics, and browser-related data (excluding sensitive passwords) to enhance Platform functionality and user experience.

Passwords are never stored in plaintext; only encrypted versions are maintained for security.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Platform’s functionality.
  • Process transactions and manage licenses for purchased resources.
  • Improve user experience through analytics and personalized recommendations.
  • Communicate with you regarding your account, purchases, or support requests.
  • Ensure compliance with our Terms and Conditions and Community Guidelines.

Under GDPR, our legal basis for processing your data includes your consent, contractual necessity (e.g., for account and payment processing), and our legitimate interests (e.g., improving the Platform).

3. Data Sharing

We do not share sensitive personal information (such as passwords or PayPal credentials) with third parties, except with necessary service providers (e.g., Paystack for payment processing) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements. We may share anonymized or aggregated data for analytics or Platform improvement.

For California residents (under CCPA), we do not sell your personal information. If you believe your data has been shared inappropriately, you may opt out of data sharing by contacting us.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Manage user sessions (essential cookies).
  • Analyze Platform performance and usage trends (analytics cookies).
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations (functional cookies).

Under GDPR, we require your explicit consent for non-essential cookies (e.g., analytics and functional cookies). You can manage your preferences via the cookie consent popup or your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Platform functionality.

For California residents, you may opt out of non-essential cookies at any time via the cookie consent settings or by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption of sensitive information (e.g., passwords and payment details handled by Paystack), to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and users are responsible for maintaining the security of their account credentials.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: Update or correct inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Data Portability (GDPR): Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Opt-Out (CCPA): Opt out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell your data).
  • Restrict Processing (GDPR): Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.
  • Object (GDPR): Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Non-Discrimination (CCPA): We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond within 30 days (GDPR) or 45 days (CCPA), as required by law.
